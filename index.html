About the product

A Rainwater Harvesting System is both a storage system and a filtration system. There have been several other inventions similar to the said project as well. But our version is to aim for both storage and filtration systems to make rainwater potable.

How it works

  1. The rainwater would collect on the roof or catchment area.
  2. The water would flow down the gutters and into downspouts.
  3. Water would go into the pipes connected to the jugs and be stored.
  4. The water in the Jug would be filtered by the LifeStraw.
  5. The water could then be used for drinking, cooking, and washing. and maintain.
